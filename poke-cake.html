Poke Cake

Description

Poke Cake is one of my favorites. My Grandmother would make it after almost every meal my family and I ate at her house.

It's a "hidden-gem" in the world of cooking and I'm happy to be sharing this recipe with you.

Ingredients you will need:

  • 1 pkg White Cake Mix
  • 1 Cup Boiling Water
  • 16 oz Container of Cool Whip
  • 1 Small Pkg Jello
  • 1/2 Cup Cold Water

Step-By-Step

  1. Prepare the cake as directed by the box.
  2. Let cake cool for 15 minutes once complete.
  3. Preparer the Jello, again following the directions found on the box.
  4. Poke the cooled cake with a for at 1/2" intervals.
  5. Sppon or gently pour the Jello mixture evenly over the top of the cake.
  6. Chill for 3-4 hours and top with Cool Whip to serve

Congrats! You have made Poke Cake! Enjoy!
